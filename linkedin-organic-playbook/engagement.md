# Engagement Strategy

How to interact with the feed and your community to amplify brand presence.

### The "Draft Off Giants" Commenting Method
Scaling an audience from zero relies heavily on visibility in other creators' comment sections.
* **Tactic:** Identify 10 leading voices in your niche. Turn on notifications and leave highly thoughtful, additive (2-3 sentence) comments within the first 15 minutes of them posting. 
* **Effect:** You borrow their reach, and the LinkedIn algorithm heavily rewards early, quality engagement.
* *(Source: [Justin Welsh](../research/sources.md), [`../research/linkedin-posts/justin-welsh.md`, Post 4: February 2026])*

### Managing Inbound Trust
Treat every comment as a micro-consultation, taking the opportunity to prove your expertise publicly instead of rushing to "take it to the DMs". A highly visible conversation drives more demand than hidden, aggressive 1:1 selling.
* *(Source: [Chris Walker](../research/sources.md), [`../research/youtube-transcripts/chris-walker.md`])*
