# Lead Capture & Demand Creation

The historical B2B enterprise playbook relied on capturing emails to "nurture" leads. The modern LinkedIn organic playbook explicitly rejects this.

### Ungate Everything
Creating friction via forms stops modern B2B buyers from consuming your content. Buyers educate themselves independently.
* **Tactical Shift:** Distribute your best frameworks, templates, and whitepapers directly in the feed without requiring an email address.
* *(Source: [Chris Walker](../research/sources.md), [`../research/linkedin-posts/chris-walker.md`])*

### Pipeline > MQLs
Stop tracking vanity marketing metrics like Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs) sourced from gated downloads, as they have an abysmal close rate and lack buying intent.
* **Tactical Shift:** Track organic pipeline contribution and self-reported attribution ("How did you hear about us?") on high-intent inbound demo requests. 
* *(Source: [Dave Gerhardt](../research/sources.md), [`../research/youtube-transcripts/dave-gerhardt-exitfive.md`])*
