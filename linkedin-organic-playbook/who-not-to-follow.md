# Who I Would NOT Recommend Following

Out of the 10 leading experts researched for this B2B SaaS analysis, there is one figure whose primary advice conflicts with long-term trust building.

### I do NOT recommend following Morgan Ingram

**Why:** 
Morgan heavily emphasizes "messaging hacks," "LinkedIn Sales Navigator tricks," and rapid outbound social selling tactics (e.g., *“How This LinkedIn Messaging Hack Made Me $120,000”*). 

In the current AI-saturated landscape of 2026, B2B buyers harbor deep resentment toward automated outreach, unsolicited DMs, and aggressive "social selling." His playbook encourages behaviors that optimize perfectly for booking immediate short-term meetings but ultimately risk severe, long-term brand damage for a B2B SaaS company aiming to build genuine inbound trust and a premier market category.
